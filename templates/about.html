Understanding Wafer Faults

In semiconductor manufacturing, especially CPU production, wafer defects can significantly impact yield and performance

Impact of Wafer Defects

Semiconductor wafers are the foundation of modern CPUs and other integrated circuits. A single 300mm wafer can contain hundreds of CPU dies, with each modern processor containing billions of transistors.

Even microscopic defects can render entire sections of a wafer unusable, directly impacting manufacturing yields and production costs.

Common Causes

  • Contamination: Dust particles and chemical residues
  • Process Variations: Inconsistencies in manufacturing
  • Material Impurities: Defects in silicon substrate
  • Handling Damage: Scratches from robotic systems
